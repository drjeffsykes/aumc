Arkansas Undergraduate Mathematics Competition AUMC

The Arkansas Undergraduate Mathematics Competition is a friendly team-based competition for students at small colleges and universities in Arkansas, usually held each year on the last Saturday in February.

2023: 18th Annual AUMC

Saturday, February 25, 2023
Hosted by John Brown University

  • 2023 Announcement Flier

2022

The 2022 competition was also cancelled due to external pressures. We also mourn the 2021 death of Dr. Gerald Heuer, who wrote and graded the AUMC exam for its first seventeen years.

2021

The 2021 competition was cancelled due to COVID-19.
